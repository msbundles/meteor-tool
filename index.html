Meteor-Tool

This site uses NASA's Open Data Portal to access data on all of the meteorite landings in the United States.
This website takes you location and gives you a list of all of the meteorites that have fallen within a certain radius of your current location.
After you input your desired radius, and click the "Get List" button. Your browser will ask for your location, and after a minute you will get your list.
